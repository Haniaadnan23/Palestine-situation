Support Palestine

The Mosque Fund is committed to the protection of Islamic houses of prayer all over the world and the local communities that worship in them. We firmly stand against oppression in all its forms and believe in justice for communities fighting against foreign invasions. Your support will be disbursed to organizations in Palestine that will serve the following purposes:


  • Relief for Palestinians injured as a result of recent violent clashes

  • Relief for the families of deceased Palestinians as a result of recent violent clashes

  • Restoration of the Al-Aqsa Mosque Compound

  • Restoration of the Al-Asqa Mosque interior
